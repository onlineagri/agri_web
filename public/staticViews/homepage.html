What's Deals@click?

Our primary motto behind this project is to provide the local businessmen a platform for ease of business, marketing and deliver great deals. India is digitizing rapidly, but the he scenario with local businessmen and vendors are worsening due to ignorance of adaptability with digitization. People often buy accessories and luxuries at high or fixed prices from shopping malls, e-commerce website but bargain heavily on local markets. Deals@click, therefore as a team has taken a small initiative to give a helping hand to these people by providing them justified deals. Also, we would assist them to create a solid and satisfactory customer base via our platform.
