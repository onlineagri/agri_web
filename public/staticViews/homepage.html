What's dealsTick

Our primary motto behind this project is to provide the farmers and local businessmen a platform for ease of business, marketing and deliver great deals. The condition of farmers has been horrible since decades given that India is an agricultural country. The scenario is no different with local businessmen and vendors, people often buy accessories and luxuries at high or fixed prices from shopping malls but bargain heavily on local markets. We, therefore as a team has taken a small initiative to give a helping hand to these people by providing them justified deals. Also we would assist them to create a solid and satisfactory customer base via our platform, DealsTick.
